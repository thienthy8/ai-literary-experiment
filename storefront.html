The Experiment

We are living through a shift in how words are born. This isn't about replacing the human author — it's about exploring the middle ground where human intent meets machine intelligence to create something neither could produce alone.

Every word in these three books was shaped by a human who chose what to keep, what to cut, and what to rewrite. AI assisted. A human led. That's not a disclaimer — it's the entire point. And when the next model arrives, we'll do it again — same intent, new apprentice — so you can watch the evolution unfold.

I believe high-level AI literacy should be as accessible, refreshing, and essential as your morning cup of coffee.

Why This Works

The Three Pillars

Pillar 01

The Coffee Standard

A good book, like a good brew, should wake you up. By pegging the price to a daily ritual, we're testing a thesis: can we sustain a global library through micro-contributions? Your purchase isn't a transaction — it's fuel for the next chapter. 100% of revenue will be donated after 12 months.

Pillar 02

Radical Transparency

Every book is a snapshot of a moment in time. Living documents. As models evolve, so does the experiment. Each edition is versioned — Book 1 (v2026. Assisted by Claude 4.6) — so you can see how the same human intent produces different results as the apprentice upgrades. You get the markdown source files — the "bones" of the book. The exact prompts and structure. Use them to see how it was made, then use them to create your own experiment. Let me know how it shaped out.

Pillar 03

The Global Pulse

Intelligence has no borders. Our map tracks every "coffee" bought across the globe. Country-level only. No personal data. Ever. Just the fact that someone in a corner of the world decided to lean into the future today.
