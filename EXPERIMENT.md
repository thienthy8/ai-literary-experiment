# An AI Literary Experiment

**Status:** Brainstorm
**Intent:** Awareness, not revenue. Trust artifact, not product.

---

## The Opening

**Prompt alone does not create outcome.**

These three books are proof. Every word was shaped by a human who chose what to keep, what to cut, and what to rewrite. AI assisted. A human led. That's not a disclaimer — it's the entire point.

I believe high-level AI literacy shouldn't be locked behind a paywall. It should be as accessible, refreshing, and essential as your morning cup of coffee.

Welcome to the experiment.

---

## The Three Pillars

### 1. Knowledge as a Commodity (The Coffee Standard)

Why the price of a coffee? Because a good book, like a good brew, should wake you up.

By pegging the price to a daily ritual, we're testing a thesis: **Can we sustain a global library through micro-contributions?** Your purchase isn't just a transaction — it's fuel for the next chapter.

100% of revenue will be donated after 12 months to an organization that advances literacy. The org will be selected transparently, and the selection itself becomes part of the story.

### 2. Radical Transparency

Every book in this series — *Meet AI*, *Own AI*, and *Leverage AI* — is a snapshot of a moment in time. Living documents. As the models evolve, so does the experiment.

**What you get when you buy:**
- Access to the interactive book (permanent Lovable link)
- The `.md` source files — the raw material, yours to study, remix, and experiment with

Why give away the markdown? Because the point isn't protecting the output. It's proving that **human intent + AI capability = something neither produces alone.** The source files are the evidence.

### 3. The Global Pulse

Intelligence has no borders. The Interactive Sales Map tracks every "coffee" bought across the globe — **country-level only, all-time, no personal data.**

We don't track who you are or exactly where you live — just the fact that someone in a specific corner of the world decided to lean into the future today.

## Thesis Testing

**Core Question:** Can micro-priced, AI-assisted books build a self-sustaining awareness engine?

### What We're Measuring

| Signal | How | Success Looks Like |
|--------|-----|-------------------|
| Coffee-price conversion | Track purchases vs. visitors | >3% conversion rate |
| Gift behavior | "Buy for someone" vs. "buy for myself" | >15% gift purchases |
| Geographic reach | Global Pulse map data | 10+ countries in 30 days |
| Bundle preference | Single vs. all-three | >40% choose bundle |
| Markdown downloads | Track .md file access | >50% of buyers download |
| Repeat engagement | Same buyer purchases second/third book | >20% return rate |

### Price Sensitivity (Future Test)
- A/B test $3 / $5 / $7 once there's baseline traffic
- Consider pay-what-you-want with $5 suggested minimum
- Regional pricing may not be needed at coffee-scale — $5 is globally accessible

### The Credibility Question
At $5, does a C-suite reader take this seriously? **The donation model neutralizes this.** "Free knowledge, funded by coffee" reads differently than "cheap book." The markdown files add substance — you're not buying a pamphlet, you're getting the laboratory notebook.

## Lovable Implementation Notes

### DATA INTEGRITY RULE — READ THIS FIRST

**Every interactive data feature on this site must be triggered ONLY by a verified Stripe payment.** This applies to:

1. **Global Pulse** (map + stats counters) — A country illuminates and counters increment only when a Stripe `checkout.session.completed` webhook fires successfully.
2. **Morning Coffee Relay** (live feed) — A new feed entry appears only when a verified purchase is recorded in the `purchases` table via Stripe webhook. No simulated, seeded, or placeholder entries in production.
3. **Whisper Wall** (reader guestbook) — The submission form is accessible only on the post-purchase confirmation page, gated by a valid Stripe session ID. No anonymous submissions. No public-facing form.
4. **Confidence Shift** (poll) — The poll is accessible only on the post-purchase confirmation page, gated by a valid Stripe session ID. One vote per purchase.

**Why this matters:** The experiment's credibility depends on authentic data. Fabricated activity — even well-intentioned placeholder data — would undermine the radical transparency pillar and erode trust. If there are zero purchases, the map shows zero countries, the relay is empty, and the counters read 0. That honesty *is* the brand.

**Implementation:** All four features read from the same `purchases` Supabase table, which is populated exclusively by Stripe webhooks. The write path is: Stripe webhook → server-side verification → Supabase insert. There is no client-side write path. The storefront HTML prototype includes mock data for design purposes only — none of it should carry over to production.

### Payment Flow (Stripe Checkout)

**Trigger:** When a reader clicks "Buy Book + Source Files" (single, $5) or "Get All Three" (bundle, $10), a Stripe Checkout session opens.

**Stripe Checkout configuration:**
- **Line item name:** "AI Literary Experiment" (for single: append book title, e.g., "AI Literary Experiment — Meet AI: First Words")
- **Prices:** $5 per single book, $10 for the bundle (all three)
- **Payment methods:** All Stripe-supported (card, Apple Pay, Google Pay, etc.)
- **Billing country:** Collected automatically by Stripe. This is the ONLY data point used for all live features (Global Pulse map, Morning Coffee Relay, Whisper Wall, Confidence Shift). No additional location tracking.
- **Receipt:** Stripe sends an automatic email receipt. Line item displays as "AI Literary Experiment."
- **No accounts required.** No login. No registration. One-time payment only.

### Post-Purchase Delivery

After successful payment, the reader is redirected to a **confirmation/delivery page** that includes:

1. **Book access links** (based on what was purchased):
   - Meet AI: First Words → https://meet-ai-first-words.lovable.app/
   - Own AI → https://own-ai.lovable.app/
   - Leverage AI → https://leverage-ai.lovable.app/
   - Bundle buyers receive all three links.

2. **Markdown source file downloads** (based on what was purchased):
   - Meet AI → `book1-meet-ai-concept-final.md`
   - Own AI → `book2-ai-and-you-concept-final.md`
   - Leverage AI → `book3-still-you-concept-final.md`
   - Bundle buyers receive all three files.
   - Files are hosted in the repo under `source-files/` and served as direct downloads.

3. **Whisper Wall prompt** — "After reading, I gave myself permission to ___." (gated by Stripe session ID)

4. **Confidence Shift poll** — Before/After feeling selector (gated by Stripe session ID)

**Delivery email (optional future enhancement):** Send a follow-up email via Stripe with the book links and download links, in case the reader closes the confirmation page.

### Interactive World Map (Global Pulse)
- **Type:** Choropleth world map (Mapbox, Leaflet, or react-simple-maps)
- **Data source:** Supabase table `purchases` with columns: `{country_code, timestamp, book_id}`
- **Privacy:** Country-level ONLY. Derived from Stripe billing address country. **No PII stored. No city. No name. No email in map data.**
- **Behavior:** Countries illuminate (fill with accent color) when at least 1 purchase exists. Hover shows reader count for that country.
- **Stats bar:** Total readers, total countries, total $ in coffees. All-time cumulative.
- **100 Countries Goal:** Progress bar showing countries / 100. Milestone celebrations at 25, 50, 75, 100.

### Morning Coffee Relay (Live Feed)
- **Data source:** Same `purchases` Supabase table, ordered by timestamp DESC
- **Privacy:** Messages ONLY use country name. Format: "Someone in [Country] joined the experiment &#9749;"
- **No personas, no cities, no demographics.** We only know country from Stripe.
- **Allowed variations:** "joined the experiment" / "gifted a book" / "got the complete experiment"
- **Real-time:** Supabase realtime subscriptions or polling every 30s
- **Fallback:** If no recent purchases, show the most recent 8 from all-time

### Whisper Wall (Reader Guestbook)
- **Submission mechanism:** Post-purchase only. After Stripe payment confirms, show a single-field form:
  - Prompt: "After reading, I gave myself permission to ___."
  - Country dropdown (pre-filled from Stripe billing country)
  - Book selector (pre-filled from purchase)
  - One submission per purchase (tied to Stripe session ID to prevent spam)
- **No login. No account. No email collected for this feature.**
- **Moderation:** Queue new whispers for manual approval before display (Supabase `whispers` table with `approved` boolean)
- **Supabase table:** `{id, text, country_code, book_id, stripe_session_id, approved, created_at}`
- **Display:** Masonry layout, randomized order, paginated (load more)

### Confidence Shift Poll
- **Trigger:** Post-purchase page, below the whisper prompt
- **4 options before:** Anxious / Overwhelmed / Skeptical / Curious
- **4 options after:** Informed / Capable / Empowered / Inspired
- **One vote per purchase** (tied to Stripe session ID)
- **Supabase table:** `{id, before_feeling, after_feeling, book_id, stripe_session_id, created_at}`
- **Display:** Live percentages calculated from aggregate data

### Book Versioning
- **Format:** `v{YEAR}` + `Assisted by {Model Name}`
- **Current:** v2026, Assisted by Claude 4.6
- **Appears on:** Book cover overlay, version badge below title, Apprentice Changelog section
- **Future editions:** When a new version is published, previous versions remain accessible. The storefront shows the latest by default with a "Previous editions" link.
- **Design for expansion:** Book grid uses `auto-fit` with `minmax(260px, 1fr)` — adding Book 4, 5, etc. will reflow naturally

### Apprentice Changelog
- **Timeline UI:** Vertical timeline, left-bordered
- **Current entries:** v2026 (active), v2027 (planned/dimmed), 2028+ (future/dimmed)
- **Updated annually** when new editions are published. Each entry describes what changed in how the AI expressed ideas — not a technical changelog, but a *human* one.

## The Strategic Frame

This experiment isn't a revenue play. It's a **trust engine.**

The books demonstrate:
- Deep AI understanding (substance)
- Ability to translate complexity into clarity (the T Ngo superpower)
- Generosity with knowledge (open-source markdown, 100% donation)
- Practice-what-you-preach (human-led, AI-assisted)

**The flywheel:** Book credibility → professional conversations → advisory engagements. The coffee price removes friction. The donation removes suspicion. The markdown files remove gatekeeping. What remains is pure signal.
